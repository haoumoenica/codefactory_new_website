Our courses

With a variety of courses at your disposal, we ensure that you are well prepared for the job market. Our Alumni success in their careers after the bootcamp is something we pride ourselves with, with 80% of graduates finding a developer job within two months after graduating.
Our courses range from basic HTML/CSS and JavaScript, to get introduced to coding, to intensive frontend, backend and fullstack development. The frontend course includes TypeScript and Angular, the backend focuses on PHP, Symfony and MySQL, and the fullstack contains all of those technologies.
With the supportive help of our excellent trainers, learning how to code has never been within your reach! You will also get the chance to start new friendships and bond with a community you can call your own.
Join us at CodeFactory and discover that we mean it when we say coding is for everybody!
